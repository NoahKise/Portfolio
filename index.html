I grew up in Minneapolis, but these days I call Portland, Oregon home. My background is predominantly in the service industry- I’ve spent the last decade working every job there is, from security to cooking to serving to bartending to management. During that time I attended Portland State University and graduated in 2014 with a bachelors degree in Sociology. More recently, I reached a point in my life where I realized I needed a career change, and coding is something that has always intrigued me. I plan to bring all the interpersonal knowledge and soft skills I’ve gained over the years working with customers and studying societal structures to the tech sector, to explore the human side of the internet.
